Where does my name come from?

Often I am asked where my name comes from. If you like to know more, please check out the information about the name von Laszewski.

The information refers to the early use of the name related to a Teutonic knight called Zelberschwecht. At one point in history, they took on the name of the town Laszewo where they used to live.
